iCELLis® Bioreactors
The iCELLis® system is a single use fixed bed bioreactor for adherent cell growth on macrocarriers. The fixed bed provides a 3D environment with low shear stress resulting in very high cell densities and high productivity. The iCELLis 500 is designed for full cGMP compliance and is therefore appropriate for manufacturing environments.
Xpansion® Multiplate Bioreactor System
Used for the production of cell therapy clinical material, the Xpansion Bioreactor can be rapidly deployed within less stringent infrastructure requirements, leading to a substantial reduction of investment and operational costs. The design of the Xpansion system is based on stacked plates made from the same materials as more traditional 2-D multi-tray stacks – minimizing the need for adaptation, and allowing for easier, more straightforward transfer.
